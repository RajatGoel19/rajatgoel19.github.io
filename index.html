01 — About

Data science with a clinical purpose

I'm a Healthcare Data Scientist with 10+ years of experience turning complex clinical and administrative data into insights that drive quality improvement, regulatory compliance, and operational efficiency across acute-care and Medicaid settings. I currently lead nursing analytics at St. Peter's Health Partners, building ML models that optimize care delivery while keeping fairness and transparency at the center of clinical decisions.

My work spans readmission risk stratification, Medicaid compliance automation with Microsoft Copilot agents, and large-scale clinical analytics in Python, SQL, Azure, Snowflake, and Tableau — with a track record of 50% efficiency gains through AI automation and 89% accuracy in predictive healthcare models, always under 100% HIPAA adherence. I'm also the co-founder of ToraTax, an AI-powered tax platform serving 3,000+ monthly visitors at 95% accuracy.

I'm currently pursuing a PhD in Artificial Intelligence, building on my Master's in Data Science from Clarkson University (4.0 GPA). Alongside my research, I contribute to the field as a peer reviewer for the Baylor University Medical Center Proceedings and a technical reviewer for Manning Publications.
